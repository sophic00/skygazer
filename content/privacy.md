I use a self-hosted [Umami](https://umami.is) instance for analytics. The data lives on my own server and no third party ever sees it.

**What I collect:** page views, referrer URLs, device type, browser, OS, and country.

**What I don't:** cookies, IP addresses, or anything personally identifiable.

I don't sell or share any of this. I just like seeing which posts people find interesting.
